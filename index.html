About Human Disease Identification System using API Integration

This project is a comprehensive web-based disease identification system that integrates with professional medical APIs to provide accurate symptom analysis and disease prediction. By leveraging advanced API technology and medical databases, the system offers reliable preliminary health assessments based on user-reported symptoms.

Founded by a team of healthcare professionals and AI experts, we combine medical expertise with advanced technology to provide accurate, reliable, and quick health insights.
